## Haozhe Zhang

**Modeling Engineer at Tesla — LLMs & physics-based ML.**

I work where physics-based modeling meets machine learning, on next-generation battery cells: theoretical and computational modeling, RL for automation, and data-driven optimization at scale-up. PhD in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Virginia, BS from USTC — my doctoral work centered on theoretical and computational modeling of multi-physics fields.

Outside the day job I keep building — LLMs, agentic systems, RL training infrastructure, voice agents for narrow domains, and AI for hardware. Mostly self-taught, and usually shipped end-to-end from the math to a working system.
